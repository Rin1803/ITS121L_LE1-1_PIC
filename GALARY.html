PERFECT VELVET

Red Velvets' second full-length album, Perfect Velvet, has the song Peek-A-Boo as the title track. It was released on November 17, 2017, almost two and a half years till their last full album release, The Red. The album received generally positive reviews internationally and in their native country, South Korea, emphasizing the group's musical experimentation and future-oriented sound.

The Perfect Velevt album is just like the name says, under the velvet era of the group, where it primarily features hip-hop, soul, and R&B genres. The album's first song is the title track, Peek-A-Boo . The rest are Look, I Just, Kingdom Come, My Second Date, Attaboy, Peerfect 10, About Love, and Moonlight Melody.

The title track, Peek-A-Boo from the Perfect Velvet album, is an exciting and catchy song with an accompanying music video that displays Red Velvet's distinctive and trendy idea, with a creative music video aswell. Other tunes on the CD include Look, I Just, and Kingdom Come, among others, each with a distinct musical taste.

RUSSIAN ROULETTE

Red Velvets' Third Mini Album, Russian Roulette, was released on the 7th of September, 2017. The Mini album consists of 7 songs with the title track that share the same name as the album. This album falls under the Red category of red velvet, where they focus more on experimental and pop music.

The title track, Russian Roulette , is one of the Fan favorite title tracks to this day. The lyrics of Russian Roulette metaphorically compare the uncertainties of love to a dangerous game of Russian Roulette. The music video features vibrant and colorful visuals, reflecting Red Velvet's signature style under the Red concept.

THE REVE FESTIVAL FINALE

'The ReVe Festival' Finale is a Red Velvet repackage album. It was released on December 23, 2019, with the title track Psycho as the first single. It is the third and final installment of the 'The ReVe Festival' trilogy. This repackaged album consists of all the previous 12 songs for the past 2 REVE FESTIVAL Mini Albums and four additional new pieces.

The title track of this album is Psycho. This title track is among the most highly acclaimed songs under Red Velvet's Name. The 16-track track list was revealed again on December 15, 2019, at midnight, revealing four new tracks, Psycho, In & Out, Remember Forever coming from the album

A special track La Rouge that were performed during their titular third concert on November 23-24, 2019 at Korea University's Hwa-jung Tiger Dome in Seoul, South Korea. The Mini album Queendom is a collection of six songs that blend numerous genres such as dance-pop, R&B, and soul. The title track of this album has the same name as the album Queendom. The website of the album is Pose, Knock on Wood, Better BE, Pushin N Pullin, Hello Sunset
